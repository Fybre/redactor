Webhook Payload Templates

Jinja2 templates that control the exact JSON body posted to a webhook. When a job uses a template, the rendered output replaces the standard payload — perfect for direct Therefore or other DMS integration without an adapter.

Available variables: job_id, filename, stem (filename without extension), status, level, page_count, entities_found, total_entities, processing_ms, completed_at, file_data (base64), file_name, file_size_bytes. Jinja2 filters like | default(value) and | tojson are supported.
